      4. charliechaplin.com - Charlie Chaplin official website

      The homepage invites the user with a photo of Charlie Chaplin
      in the left foreground and 3 women in the background to his right.
      Chaplin's signature floats about the heads of the women.

      Proximity is used to group 4 categories: Biography, Films, Lyrics & Speeches and Products.


      Biography:

      Overview of His Life, Charlie’s Mother(Hannah Chaplin), Charlie Chaplin’s Wives, and Chaplin as a Composer

      Films:

      The Circus, City Lights, Modern Times, and Limelight

      Lyric & Speeches:

      The Final Speech from the Great Dictator, Smile Lyrics, Nonsense Song from Modern Times (Titine), and Speech from Monsieur Verdoux

      Products:

      My Autobiography, The Circus DVD & Blue-ray, Modern Times DVD & Blue-ray, Various Items
